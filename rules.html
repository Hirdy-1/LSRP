Case file 02 — standing orders

The rulebook.

Every rule currently enforced in Los Santos Roleplay, split into general conduct, civilian RP restrictions, and banned RP. This list is also pinned in Discord — that copy is always the source of truth.

Part one

General rules.

01

No RDM.

02

No VDM.

03

No powergaming.

04

No metagaming.

05

No ERP.

06

No FRP.

07

No cop baiting.

08

No civ baiting.

09

No armoured vehicles.

10

Keep maps off at all times.

11

Keep driving realistic and RP out car crashes.

12

Be sure to follow fearRP and NVL rules at all times — be scared if your life is threatened.

13

Keep RP clean and without any form of slurs, derogatory terms, or racially charged language.

14

Be mindful of others and try not to negatively impact people's RP.

15

We love creative RP and passive businesses!

16

Stay in character at all times.

17

No mic spamming.

18

Be respectful to staff and other members at all times.

19

Trolling, intentionally ruining other people's RP, or having no intent to RP is not allowed.

Part two

Civilian RP restrictions.

01

Shooting at LEO or other members for no reason is against the rules — there must be a storyline or context. Shooting at a police officer just because they pulled you over is considered RDM.

02

You should only kill other civilians if there is a good reason and a build-up. Small disagreements are not a reason to kill other people.

03

You must never kidnap people to kill them or for no reason — no one enjoys being randomly kidnapped, driven around for 30 minutes, and then killed.

04

Do not dress in excessively military clothing, impersonate military, or look like you should be in a Call of Duty lobby.

Part three

Banned RP.

01

Controversial real-life and fictional characters (e.g. sexual predators, rapists, racists, etc.) are BANNED. Rule of thumb: if you google their name and the first page of results covers these topics, don't RP as them.

02

Covid RP is BANNED.

03

Cancer RP is BANNED.

04

Sexual RP is BANNED.

07

Hitman RP is BANNED (for now).

08

Paranormal or supernatural RP is BANNED (unbanned for Halloween events).

09

Terrorist RP is BANNED.

10

9/11 RP is BANNED.
